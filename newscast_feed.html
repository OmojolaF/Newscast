A Fun, Random, Beauty Report Straight From The Closet

Zoe Keating

6 hours ago

Three years ago, @paulodelvalle started the My Instagram Logo project, inspiring thousands of creators to remix our icon in wildly creative ways. Paulo, who lives in Sao Paulo, used more than 400 flowers to make his remix of our app's new look. "I went to the biggest flower shop in the whole of Rio de Janiero and bought half of their stock for Mother's Day. Then I got home and I had no idea how I was going to do it!" he explains. Eight hours later, he had something beautiful.
